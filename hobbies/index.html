Hobbies

I have many things that I do in my free time. Some of which involve video games, watching movies, or even running. I have been playing video games for a long time, and I have always loved to play them with my friends. I don’t really have a specific game that I enjoy playing with them the most. All I really care about is having fun and laughing it up with some good friends. I don’t just play games with my friends, sometimes I enjoy playing games on my own. Some of those games consist of Skyrim or various Pokemon games. Since I have been consistently running for a while now, it has become habit, and I am starting to run more on my own. It keeps me in shape, and gives me something to do. I’m also in shape enough already to participate in various races, such as a 5k or a half marathon. When I’m not running, playing video games, or actually being productive, you can find me spending time with friends. I always love spending time with my friends, and I do it quite often. As time goes on, I am slowly finding more things to do on my own time. Ironically I still find myself bored when I have some many things to do, and hobbies to keep up with.
